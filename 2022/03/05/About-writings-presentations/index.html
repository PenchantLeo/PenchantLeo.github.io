Writings

1º Build the Structure

  • First of all, raise the scenario vividly. (The Introdauction)
    [This will pull the reader close to you.(you can make a metaphore)]
  • What is a “whatever u wanna write”?
  • Why “whatever u wanna write” exists?
  • When “…” happens?
  • Which territory “…” stays in?
  • How does “whatever u wanna write” work?
  • The pros and cons of “whatever u wanna write”.
  • The Summary

2º Absorb

Before you write you need a lot reserves about what you gonna write.

Quantities of Jornal articles is necessity, especially those overviews.

What’s more, those old classical articles with high citations and popular new articles are same important as overviews.
(¡Las referencias de articulos tambien!)

Classical articles usually are authorities. But those new articles sometimes can bring up improvements.

These are what we should learn. About how to think critally. (This we’ll talk later)

Presentations

1º How to Start One

First word – Answer the “Why Question”

  • Why you talk your subject?
  • Why the audience liten to you?

How to answer the “Why Question” ?

TELL A STORY!!

You can start with “Yesterday I had a conversation with…”

Then “Now why do I tell you this? Because my subject is…”

2º Somethings you need to pay attention with

  • Speak LOUDER and FACE the audience.
  • Don’t just read ur draft & make EYE CONTACTS.
  • To show your confidence SMILE.
